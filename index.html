Greg Mcqueen

Perfect imperfections

"As a Documentary Wedding Photographer, I specialize in working
with couples who dislike having their picture taken. What I do is
document the wedding day as it happens, capturing real events and
emotions. I call it, perfectly imperfect wedding photography."

A picture is worth a thousand words

"From my first wedding and one of the first frames that got me
hooked on documentary wedding photography. A father tying his
son's tie on the son's wedding day. He tied and re-tied it
several times. As captured more and more images, I realized
the deeper story... A father prepping his son for life as a
husband. There are always deeper stories in the simplest of
moments when it comes to documentary wedding photography."
